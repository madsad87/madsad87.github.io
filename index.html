History

Early in the wake of the European Red Guard's [RG] success Aggression [Agg] saw the opportunity to make a name for themselves on the North American front. Red Guard proved that a small grp of organized, driven, individuals could outplay the hordes of players that roamed wildly about the battlefield in Guild Wars 2 World vs World. Jericho headlined the North American expansion of this playstyle and built a relentless frontline, hand picking not just the best and brightest, but finding those who could adapt to many situations and follow orders, even in the face of unconquerable danger. Here today, stands Aggression [Agg], Relentless by nature, battle hardened warriors, wrought of iron flesh, veins coursing with steel, true Ironborn, the dead that may never die.
